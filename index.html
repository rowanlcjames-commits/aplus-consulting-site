About

About APlus Consulting.

APlus Consulting was established in 2017 by Alistair James, Pr Eng. We provide advisory and review services to consultants, financial institutions, law firms, investors and industry, with a focus on mining, waste and water.

We work with a network of senior professionals to provide practical, commercially aware technical advice on waste, water and natural resource management. Assignments range from short-form independent reviews and due diligence work to long-term Engineer of Record appointments.

How we work

  • Reliable service that clients return to.
  • Confidentiality and respect for client intellectual property.
  • Practical, commercially aware and risk-appropriate advice.
  • Up to date with technical developments in our fields.
  • Clear value on every project.
  • Mindful of social and environmental impact in the solutions we propose.

Services

Core services.

From scoping and design through construction, operation and closure.

Tailings, heap leach and waste management

Design, monitoring and management of tailings dams, waste rock dumps and heap leach facilities from concept through closure.

  • Tailings dam design and Engineer of Record services
  • Pollution control dams and liner systems
  • Mine water use efficiency and spillage prevention
  • Capping, cover and closure design
  • Geochemical waste characterisation
  • Risk, hazard and dam safety assessments

Water resources and pollution prevention

Water management for mining and industrial sites, including pollution prevention, prediction, treatment selection and remediation.

  • Water and salt balance, facility sizing
  • ARD assessment, prevention and remediation
  • Pit lake studies, decant timing and quality predictions
  • Stormwater management and harvesting
  • Groundwater pollution interception
  • Water treatment process evaluation

Due diligence and technical reviews

Independent technical review for legal and financial due diligence teams, covering transaction support through to closure liability.

  • Independent Tailings Review Board appointments
  • Equator Principles and IFC standard compliance review
  • Permit, design and CQA documentation review
  • Closure cost estimate review and optimisation
  • Pollution remediation liability assessment
  • Dam safety, seismic and stability review

Approach

Our engineering approach.

Tailings, waste and water decisions have long timescales. We consider closure, post-mining land use and regulatory requirements from the start of each assignment.

01

Risk-based

Probabilistic and deterministic risk assessment integrated into capital, operating and closure cost estimates.

02

Closure-aware

Designs evaluated against post-mining land use and long-term liability throughout the project life.

03

Standards-aligned

Work delivered in line with the Global Industry Standard on Tailings Management, Equator Principles, IFC performance standards and applicable regulations.

04

Independent

Findings reported clearly and without bias, including unpopular conclusions where supported by the evidence.
